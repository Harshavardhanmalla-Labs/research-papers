\section{Experimental Setup}

\subsection{Pre-Registration}

The cell grid, driver/scorer methods, hypotheses H1--H3, decision rules,
and stop rules in \texttt{paper9/design/PAPER9\_PROTOCOL.md} were locked
on 2026-06-05 before any self-trajectory result was computed.

\subsection{Execution}

For each cell $K \in \{50, 200\}$ and each driver $m_{\text{drive}}$,
all 25 evaluation seeds are simulated for 6 windows. At every window,
the current state is scored with all five methods; only $m_{\text{drive}}$'s
top-$K$ selection drives the next window's evolution.

\subsection{Statistical Reporting}

Cell-mean point estimates are means across 25 evaluation seeds. 95\%
confidence intervals are percentile bootstrap with 10{,}000
resamples~\cite{efron1994introduction}. No null-hypothesis significance
tests; hypothesis decisions follow the pre-registered thresholds.
