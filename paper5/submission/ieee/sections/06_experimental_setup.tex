\section{Experimental Setup}

\subsection{Pre-Registration}

The protocol --- including research questions, hypotheses, scoring formula, ground-truth definition, seed split, baselines, metrics, and stop rules --- was fixed before any evaluation-seed result was inspected. The full pre-registration protocol accompanies the reproducibility artifact release.

\subsection{Calibration Procedure}

The scorer weights used in the primary evaluation are taken verbatim from the prior HygienePrio calibration~\cite{paper4hygieneprio}: $(\alpha, \beta, \gamma, \delta) = (0.7, 0.5, 0.1, 0.2)$. These values are not re-fitted on the present calibration seeds for the primary evaluation; reuse of the prior calibration is a deliberate methodological choice to test whether \textit{pre-existing} HygienePrio weights remain effective under temporal evolution. The H3 ablation (\S\ref{sec:results}) separately exercises a per-window grid-search recalibration on the five calibration seeds (100--104) for comparison.

\subsection{Evaluation Procedure}

The present evaluation seed range (105--129) partially overlaps HygienePrio's evaluation set (100--124) but is not identical; the Window~1 mean P@50 reported here ($0.595$) is therefore on a different seed sample than the Window~1 number reported in HygienePrio ($\approx 0.509$). The two are not direct contradictions but distinct point estimates of the same population mean under the same scorer.

For each evaluation seed $s \in \{105, \ldots, 129\}$ and each window $w \in \{1, \ldots, 6\}$:
\begin{enumerate}
\item Construct fleet state $F_w^{(s)}$ from $F_{w-1}^{(s)}$ via the evolution policy (\S5.4).
\item Score all applicable pairs with each of the five methods.
\item Compute P@$K$ for $K \in \{50, 100, 250\}$ using the per-window ground truth (\S3.2).
\item Persist the (seed, window, method, K, P@K) tuple to the frozen results CSV.
\end{enumerate}

\subsection{Statistical Reporting}

Confidence intervals shown in Fig.~\ref{fig:trajectory} are 95\% percentile bootstrap CIs over the 25 evaluation seeds with 10{,}000 resamples per (window, method, $K$) cell~\cite{efron1994introduction}. No null-hypothesis significance tests are reported; comparisons rely on CI overlap, consistent with HygienePrio. Window-level comparisons are reported per window rather than pooled across windows because pooling would obscure the temporal decay pattern that is the paper's central finding.

\subsection{Reproducibility}

The simulator, scoring code, recalibration ablation, and figure generator are released under MIT licence with the artifact. From the artifact root, \texttt{PYTHONPATH=src python3 src/run\_temporal.py} reproduces the frozen results CSV from the fixed seeds, and \texttt{PYTHONPATH=src python3 -m recalib\_ablation} reproduces the H3 ablation in Table~\ref{tab:recalibration}. The simulator depends on the HygienePrio scorer package~\cite{paper4hygieneprio}.
