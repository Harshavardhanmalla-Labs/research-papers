\section{Dataset and Sweep Grid}

\subsection{Synthetic Fleet}

All evaluations use the EEHDA synthetic fleet generator from
HygienePrio~\cite{paper4hygieneprio}, identical in structural priors to
the source benchmark and its temporal extension~\cite{paper4hygieneprio,paper5temporal}: 1{,}000 users, 830 hosts, $\sim$3{,}500 (host, CVE) pairs
at window~1, drawn from DBIR-calibrated patch lag and NVD-calibrated CVSS
distributions with $\sim$12\% of CVEs at $\mathrm{EPSS} > 0.10$ and $\sim$8\%
KEV prevalence. New CVE disclosures at $w > 1$ are drawn from the same
fixture distributions.

\subsection{Sweep Grid}

The grid spans 20 cells. Cells differ \textit{only} in $(K, \lambda)$;
all other simulation parameters (patch debt reduction $\Delta_{\mathrm{patch}} =
0.15$, EPSS random-walk $\sigma = 0.02$, telemetry drift $+0.08$/window,
recovery $-0.30$, KEV prevalence $0.08$, calibrated HRS and scorer
weights) inherit from the prior temporal-stability evaluation's pre-registration verbatim.

\subsection{Seed Protocol}

The 25 evaluation seeds (105--129) are identical to the prior temporal-stability evaluation's evaluation
set, enabling per-seed paired comparisons across cells. The five
calibration seeds (100--104) are not used in this study because no
weight re-fitting is performed --- the HygienePrio calibrated weights are
used at every cell.
