\section{Experimental Setup}

\subsection{Pre-Registration}

$\tau$ grid, hypotheses H1--H4, decision rules, and stop rules were
locked in \texttt{paper11/design/PAPER11\_PROTOCOL.md} on 2026-06-05
before any $\tau$-sweep result was computed.

\subsection{Execution}

For each $K$: cache calibration and evaluation states, pre-compute
$\Delta_w$, lag-1 weights, and offline weights per window; then for
each $\tau$ apply the adaptive decision rule and score all 25 evaluation
seeds at each window with each strategy's resulting weights.

\subsection{Statistical Reporting}

Cell-mean point estimates are means across 25 evaluation seeds. 95\%
percentile bootstrap CIs (10{,}000 resamples)~\cite{efron1994introduction}.
H1, H2, and H4 Spearman correlations are computed on five points
(one per $\tau$); the small sample limits statistical power, noted
in \S\ref{sec:threats}.
