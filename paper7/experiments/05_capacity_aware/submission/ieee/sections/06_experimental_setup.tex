\section{Experimental Setup}

\subsection{Pre-Registration}

The strategy set, $\tau_K$ vector, hypotheses H1--H4, decision rules,
and stop rules in \texttt{paper12/design/PAPER12\_PROTOCOL.md} were
locked on 2026-06-05 before any capacity-aware-detector P@50 was
inspected.

\subsection{Execution}

For each $K \in \{50, 100, 200\}$: cache calibration and evaluation
states under fixed-weight HygienePrio-full trajectory; per window
fit lag-1 and offline weights and compute $\Delta_w$; apply the six
strategies' decision rules; score all 25 evaluation seeds.

\subsection{Reporting}

Cell-mean point estimates over 25 evaluation seeds. 95\% percentile
bootstrap CIs (10{,}000 resamples). H3 Spearman computed on the
three per-K fire fractions (low statistical power, noted in
\S\ref{sec:threats}).
